Learning OpenGL

I attempted to follow the tutorials over at learnopengl.com, and familiarise myself with the pipelines and workings of this powerful API. Though I did learn a lot, mainly about graphics pipelines and the way objects are stored in memory, I was doing this alongside the heat of some nasty coursework deadlines, and dropped the project.

By the end I had used SAT to create my own collisions with any 2d polygons.

I do wish to return to this, and remake infinite pong in opengl with socket networking.

Also important to note that visual studio code cooked the formatting, and that I just had a lot of dependencies linked via vs, instead of something sensible like cmake. This is just here to show extra experience with C++, and familiarity with openGL I do not recommend you to dig through the files :(
